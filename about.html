Members of the East Side Parish Committee

Shaju Brahmakulam, Joss Brahmakulam, Derin B.D., Jomon E.I., Eljo Chakkalakkal, Jirin Joss, Joss Chittilappilly, Biju Joy, Sibu Mechery, Dinty K.A., Shaju Pallan, Joseph Pottathuparambil.
The East Side is a large section of the faithful belonging to Kottekkad Church. It functions through several units, and in each unit, activities are carried out under the leadership of a President, Secretary, and Treasurer. By actively organizing worship services and festivals, the East Side Committee continues to serve as a strength to the parish in both spiritual and social spheres.
